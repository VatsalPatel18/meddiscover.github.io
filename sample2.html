Live Process

How MedDiscover Works

1. Domain-Specific Indexing

Papers are chunked (500 tokens) and embedded using MedCPT, trained specifically on PubMed abstracts.

2. Semantic Retrieval

FAISS efficiently searches the dense vector space to find the Top-K most relevant biomedical contexts.

3. Grounded Generation

GPT-4o synthesizes the answer, strictly bound to the retrieved contexts to prevent hallucination.

Performance Metrics

Silver Benchmark Results

Evaluating 600 synthetic QA pairs across 100 papers. While general-purpose embeddings (Ada-002) provide a strong baseline, domain-specific embeddings (MedCPT) yield a statistically significant improvement in Answer Correctness (p = 5.72×10⁻¹¹).

Key Finding: MedCPT improves answer correctness by effectively capturing nuanced relationships specific to medical terminology and biological pathways, bridging the gap for specialized knowledge extraction.
