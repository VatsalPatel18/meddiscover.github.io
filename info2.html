The Biomedical Challenge

  • High-Dimensional Data

    Metabolomic datasets are complex and inherently sparse (p ≫ n).

  • Expanding Literature

    PubMed repositories scale faster than static model retraining cycles.

  • Complex Terminology

    General tokenizers fragment vital clinical terms, causing domain mismatch.

03 | Experimental Setup

Isolating the
Retrieval Effect

To guarantee fairness and prevent confounding variables, we strictly fixed the decoder generation parameters across all benchmarks.

Fixed Decoder

All primary comparisons utilize GPT-4o as the generator.

Strict Output Constraints

Outputs capped at exactly 25 tokens. This forces the model to be extremely concise and prevents verbosity from artificially inflating metrics.

What's Next for MedDiscover

06 | The Technical Roadmap

Multimodal Integration

Metabolomics relies heavily on numeric assay read-outs, LC-/GC-MS spectra, and imaging data. Future iterations will adopt multimodal embedding pipelines (vision transformers, graph networks) projected into a shared latent space for holistic querying.

Streaming Indexing

Biomedical knowledge grows too quickly for periodic, batch-style index rebuilding. Implementing streaming indexing (e.g., Kafka) will permit real-time ingestion of new PubMed studies, ensuring researchers always query the absolute latest evidence.
